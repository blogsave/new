BASIC MISTAKES OF TRADERS

Not everyone can trade to the market: the market situation can change at any second and you need to quickly adjust to it, while changing your strategy. In addition, one careless move by a trader can instantly negate everything. If you can predict when if it is almost impossible to change the market, then it is much easier to avoid fatal mistakes. ERROR # 1. THE MORE INDICATORS, THE BETTER

Most novice traders use it in trading technical indicators, trying to load as much as possible. But this approach is fundamentally wrong. In the process, the indicators will only contradict each other, and the player's attention will be scattered.

TRADER'S ADVICE!For a better assessment of the market situation, use no more than three indicators at a time.

By the way, indicators can be divided into several groups â€” trend indicators, oscillators, and volume indicators. Modern technical analysis programs use variations of the main indicators, but their essence does not change. Therefore, before choosing what to add to the terminal, test indicators from all groups and select software one of each of them.

PLEASE NOTE!The selected indicators must match your trading style.

From the trend indicators, I can advise an exponential moving average, from the oscillators, the "relative strength index" will suit, and the best indicator of volumes will be the classic Â«VolumesÂ». All other indicators in this group are derivatives.

ERROR # 2. I WILL NOT CHANGE THE STANDARD SETTINGS

After selecting indicators, change the standard parameters according to your trade. In settings, there are usually average values that you can use to achieve this goal certain result. Make adjustments to increase your potential profit margin.

For example, when trading at medium-term intervals, you should focus on a moving average with a period of one hundred days or more. If you trade within a day, you will only need to set a period of 7 days.

ADVICE A TRADER!If you plan to robotize the system, pay special attention to setting up the price averaging period. You can test its performance with various indicator settings in the free version of TsLab.

Some traders choose an averaging period equal to the number of bars inside one period. For example, during a week of trading on the Russian stock market, you can see about forty hourly bars. The same applies to oscillators: adjust the standard values according to your system. For example, in the RSI indicator, in addition to the averaging period, you should select the levels displayed on the chart. Some traders choose levels 20 and 80, others-30 and 70.

PLEASE NOTE!Reducing the lower value and increasing the upper one will make the system more accurate on small timeframes.

ERROR # 3: SHOULD I USE OSCILLATORS ON A TREND CHART?!

As we know, the price chart is usually it is located in two alternative States-trend or sideways. When using technical indicators in your trading, take into account the condition of the trading instrument.

TRADER'S ADVICE!Do not use oscillators on the trend chart and use trend indicators in the flat. This approach will quickly "eat" your deposit.

Consider the following example. On the chart of the EURUSD pair, we see a great trend, where the moving average copes with its purpose, but the RSI oscillator gives false signals, in particular, it offers to enter the short, after which the pair reaches new levels maxima's.

Some use support and resistance zones in their trading. This information shows the presence of limit buyers and sellers, which is important in the process of trading.

But there is one thing: traders often get the definition wrong these zones, reducing the likelihood of profitable transactions. Definitions of support and resistance zones can be found in any textbook on technical analysis in various variations, but still their essence boils down to the following: the true support or resistance zone is that area on the chart from which the price has repeatedly pushed back, making significant movements. This movement also makes the area a zone. Without taking this point into account, novice traders draw a large number of pseudo-supports and pseudo-resistances on the chart.

ERROR # 4: USING THE SAME INDICATORS ON DIFFERENT PLATFORMS MARKETS

An important indicator used in trading is the volume of transactions. There are a huge number of systems based on the use of trading volumes, one of which is VSA (volume spread analis). Traders often use it both on the Moscow stock exchange and on the Forex market.

PLEASE NOTE ATTENTION!The volume of foreign exchange transactions is almost not available. What you see in the terminal is approximate data.

Following the "volumes" indicator on Forex and using the VSA system, you can get significant losses. To avoid losses, keep track of volume indicators for currency futures, listed on the Chicago Mercantile exchange. The necessary data can be found on the website www.cmegroup.com . Despite the fact that the information appears there with a fifteen-minute delay, you can easily use it for trading.

ERROR # 5: WE ARE TESTING SYSTEMS WITHOUT TAKING INTO ACCOUNT COMMISSIONS AND FEES SLIPPAGES

Sometimes traders believe that the most important thing is to get a profitable system, forgetting about the Commission. Although for a year of active trading, you can lose a significant part of the profit on commissions. This is true in the case of slippage

TRADER'S ADVICE!Choose a pricing plan that matches your trade.

From my personal experience, when developing several Forex strategies for starting a PAMM account in Alpari, I did not write possible slippages and widening spreads into the system. The system promised to show a yield of about 40% in three months, but in practice it showed only a minus. It's all the fault of slippage. My advice to you is to keep track of these moments in your trading.

MISTAKE # 6: LACK OF REACTION TO THE CHANGING MARKET SITUATION

Even the best strategy can one day start working against you. You need to be prepared for the fact that the market may change and you will have to quickly make adjustments to the current situation. system. Something similar was experienced by market participants in December 2014, when sharply increased volatility in the USDRUB pair made unprofitable trading systems that had previously worked perfectly well.

By the way, sometimes you can hear that the historical data you can use it to create a profitable trade, but in this case, do not forget that the market is constantly changing and the winner will be the one who will adapt to the changed situation in time.

If you follow the tips described above, you will really improve your trading, but do not forget about the psychological aspect. More we will discuss this topic in detail in the next article.
