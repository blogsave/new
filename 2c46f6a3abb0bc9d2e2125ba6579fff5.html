Morning REVIEW

Good day to all!

A fairly impressive rebound was shown by the us stock market, which has set a record for the past 5 months, after the statements of fed Chairman Powell on the readiness to ease monetary policy and the news about the readiness of Mexico to accept US proposals on migration. Nu, a corrective pullback of prices to the top has matured, as we wrote about it in the morning review yesterday. The market remains very newsy and any tweet by trump or his careless statement can spoil the mood of all markets. But for now, and it must be admitted that trump will not wish it-it is being fulfilled. I wanted to ease the monetary policy last year politicians from the fed and now six months have passed and the fed is ready to cut rates, decided to correct the US trade balance – and now the whole world is trembling from trade wars. "The magician" in one word……

Currencies – E VRO-dollar, continues its ascent. We can't rule out a decline to the 1.12 level yet , but in the future, we will be able to if this support is tested, the growth of this currency pair will continue with the targets of 1.1350 .

Dollar-ruble, given the external background and the dynamics of prices for other commodity currencies, today we should expect a strengthening of the ruble, and apparently the quotes will decline closer to 64.80. Today, the Ministry of Finance places OFZ and apparently the results of these auctions will be good. The resistance on the dollar-ruble pair will now be around 65.30

Index-Futures for the S&P500 index yesterday "exceeded the growth plan" and actually reached the level of one day of already strong resistance near the 2823 level . Today, at least there will be an attempt to test this value, but with a high probability growth will stop near this mark. Yes, yesterday we expected the index to rise to the level of 2780 , but the euphoria from the positive news of the fed and Mexico the wave that swept the markets pushed US stock index prices to higher values. We believe that the growth of the indices should stop today.

Our indices went into a correction yesterday. Strong growth over the past days should have at least stopped, we wrote about the increase in the probability of a correction yesterday in the review. For today, an attempt will probably be made to storm the annual and historical highs of the MICEX index. The RTS index, against the background of the strengthening of the ruble, should also grow. But it is better, on the growth of indices, to look for moments to sell

Raw materials – Oil prices are trying to recover and while the chances of growth to the level of b 63.40 dollars per barrel remain. After a rapid fall in prices last week, we should expect a recovery in prices. This corrective growth will remove the accumulated oversold conditions and open the way for a new decline in oil prices

Precious metals-Gold and silver are consolidating near highs for this week at 1330 and 14.80 dollars on the background of a weakening dollar and an increase in the likelihood of a rate cut in the US. While we believe such rapid growth should slow down a little at current levels, we should wait for a correction with targets around 1315 for gold . Similar in silver the situation is similar with gold and today the support is located around 14.65.
