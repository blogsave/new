7 important rules when the stock market falls

February was a hot month on the stock market due to the rapid decline of indices. Many investors began to sell off their assets and record a loss. Is it true are falls in the stock market scary?
After all what is for one is sorrow for another is joy.

7 important rules to know when the market falls:

Rule 1: falls (corrections) in the market are naturally

A decline of 15-20% for our market is considered a correction and for 23 years it has happened 35 times, i.e. 1.5 times a year.

For the American market, the decline will be considered from 10%, because this market is more Mature and established.

It happens about 1 time a year. On average, the decline lasts about 3 months (50-60 days').

Rule 2: only every 5-6 falls turn into a bear market

When the market falls, newcomers are tempted to sell all their shares, but this action is wrong. Yes, markets are falling faster than they are rising.

And if you sell your assets at this moment, then do not Wake up participate in the new growth of the market, because about 80% of all falls in the market are short-term phenomena. And by making the decision to sell some or all of your assets, you are denying yourself the opportunity to be in the long-term growth phase of the stock market.

Rule 3 : no one in the world can tell exactly when there will be a rise and fall market's

Despite the huge number of "vangs" in the market, none of them can predict the fall or growth of the stock market. Yes, and in General, this is a pointless and unnecessary occupation.
Falling markets and the beginning of the crisis already promise at least 2-3 years, but they will ever guess, because no one has canceled economic cycles, but no one knows when this will happen.

The fewer such stupid predictions we make, the better our results will be.

Rule 4: Despite possible declines, the market is growing in the long run

Declines in the market are frequent phenomena. They can be caused by a bunch of reasons from profit-fixing, a deteriorating economy to just fears of private investors.
For example, the Moscow stock exchange index fell by an average of 26.6% over the course of the year. But despite such declines, shares grew in 16 cases out of 23, i.e. in 70% of cases, the market went out of control at the end of the year. plus.

For example, the American market on average falls by 14%, but in 75% of cases, the market went to plus at the end of the year and brought an average income of 8.2%

Rule 5: every 5 years or So, the market falls

Our market is still too young to conduct such an analysis.
But if you take the US market, then for 105 years of its history, the bear market has been observed 34 times. The duration of the fall ranged from 45 to 696 days, but averaged about 1 year.


Rule 6: market Declines don't last forever

In times of crisis, many people think that this is the end of the world and the market will never grow.

But after such a fall, which was in 2008, the time of bull markets came and those who took advantage of this fall increased their capital tenfold. This applies to all markets.

Remember the seasons change neither winter nor summer are eternal!

Rule 7: To Be out of the market - this is the biggest mistake of an investor

Over the past 20 years, the yield on the US stock market has been 8.2%, despite all its terrible falls.

The average yield of our market since its inception, and there were a couple of defaults, was 29.73%.

But if you miss just 10 successful days on the market your the yield will fall by 2 times. And if you miss 30 successful days in the market, then the yield will be zero.

Keep these rules and always reread them when the market falls. Of course it is profitable and cool to buy the market on at the very bottom, but no one knows when and at what price it will be. Start investing now, don't wait for a better moment!

Learn statistics and learn math.part of it! All successful investments!
