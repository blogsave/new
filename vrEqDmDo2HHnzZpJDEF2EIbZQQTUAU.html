Use of take profit in Forex trading operations.

To begin with, let's figure out how important a trading tool is a take profit. It swerves on the goals, result, and profit of the trader. Take profit is indirectly related to everything that allows you to make a profit in trading. Need correctly calculate your own goals, be able to correctly set take profit to ensure a normal profit in Forex trading.

What is a take profit?

Limiting profits-this should be immediately remembered. Initially, many people are interested in the relevance of using take profit in their work. After all, in the situations with a stop loss are all unambiguous: you need to bet, otherwise, you can lose the entire Deposit. And what to do for normal profit fixing?

Many novice traders specifically panic. How will the price move? Not quite clear, no one knows! A random value is selected, for example, 40 points or more they set a take profit. All, the profit is secured, because the novice is sure that everything will go smoothly. In most cases, there is a disappointment, because the transaction ends in losses. Why do such moments happen?

And this simply cannot be done! Take profit is your main goal. The point where the price will appear. Your extreme point of view of the market. You build the plan up to the very point where the profit will be set. If there is no such point, there is also no plan, and you use random numbers and values, which often leads to losses.

There are plenty of such examples in our simple life, you act on the situation, nothing happens you plan, you don't set any goals, so everything seems stupid and meaningless.

When opening a trade, make sure to place a take profit!

For beginners: always set a take profit, there are no exceptions to this rule. The tool is proven and reliable effective.

Where do I place my take profit?

You need to set it at the point where your vision ends. Let's look at an example of a stop loss: you set it in a place that you are not sure of initially. Take profit should be placed at the extreme point your vision of the situation. For example, you look at the horizon and see a point that seems extreme, there is nothing further, that's where you need to put take profit.

How to calculate this point

Novice traders will initially have quite a difficult time, because they see very little. Gradually you will begin to to see the horizon and pick a good take profit levels. A great opportunity to learn more about the market. It is important not only to see, but to understand why we are reaching a certain point. There should be not just goals, but prerequisites for why the price will reach this point. You must be sure that there is nothing beyond this point.

I I understand that, for example, the fifth wave will go now. It will reach that point (most likely). And then the devil knows what will happen: maybe it will go further, perhaps even down. I do not understand. That's where I'll exit the position, and that's where I'll take a take profit.

Let's assume that the trade goes your way and the “plus” is 100 points. You we are sure that the situation has changed significantly while the price has moved to this point, which means that the market has changed. And you move the take profit to the future horizon. This will continue until you realize that you are making a mistake and you need to exit the trade, or it will work out the established limit tool.

Do you always need to use take profit or can you also fix everything manually?

This point is incredibly important. If you don't understand if anything happens, fix the transaction. I was often killed by blind faith in success, even when the market turned, I hoped that my goal would be achieved, and this lost a lot of money.

As soon as the situation deviates from the original plan – fix the profit, even if small. Then you can go back to the deal again, but no longer you will face losses. A small profit is better than no profit at all.

Is it relevant to leave a trade without a set take profit?

Of course Yes, if you are at the point where the original plan is canceled. In some situations 20 is not enough points before the set take profit, you are sure that everything will go perfectly and you will be able to fix the maximum. There is a complete reversal of the market and you lose money. This is not a problem with the analysis and its quality, but the original forecast has completely lost its relevance.

Every trader has their own rules goals, loss limits. You need to adjust trading only in a situation when the market changes dramatically and the original plan ceases to work.Stop-loss moves very rarely, many people here work under strict restrictions, but with take profit everything is different. Of course, initially we set a certain goal, we are sure that, that you can walk to it. But the situation can change dramatically and the plan will fail, you always need to consider such possibilities.

Show loyalty when working with take profit: never be greedy, take into account the relevance of your plan in order to move the profit in a timely manner. If the tool has its own name it works out, use it for maximum profit. As soon as we notice problems – we immediately jump out and search for a new point.
